Safety Protocols

🔥

Fire Emergency

  • Call 911 immediately
  • Evacuate the building
  • Stay low to avoid smoke
  • Do not use elevators
  • Meet at the assembly point
🏥

Medical Emergency

  • Call the RHU hotline
  • Do not move the patient
  • Keep the patient conscious
  • Apply basic first aid
  • Wait for responders
🚨

Crime / Security

  • Call PNP 117
  • Do not confront suspects
  • Move to a safe location
  • Document if safe to do so
  • Report to Barangay Tanod
